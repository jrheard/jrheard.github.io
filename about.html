About Me

Hi! My name’s JR Heard. I’m a staff engineer with a focus on backend webdev. I make engineering organizations better by identifying difficult technical+social problems and solving them. I think pure functions are the greatest. I live in Portland with my cat Frank and partner Keely.

I like breaking big problems into little pieces, and I like shipping each little piece to customers as soon as it’s finished. I like to explain programming-related concepts to people who are interested in learning about them. I like asking questions. Here’s my resume.

I’m always interested in learning about how a programmer can make a dent in the fields of transportation, energy/climate, and education. You can reach me at jrrrheard@gmail.com.
